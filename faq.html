How much does it cost? +
We work on simple per-item pricing with no call-out fees. Rates can shift slightly based on volume and environment (office vs. construction). Share your item count and any RCDs—we’ll send a tailored estimate within 24 hours.
What areas do you cover? +
We service the entire Sunshine Coast and nearby regions. Not sure if your site is within reach? Send the address and we’ll confirm travel at no additional cost.
How long will testing take? +
Timing depends on access and quantity. As a guide, we typically test 20–30 items per hour in an office environment. We’ll map the schedule with you beforehand so downtime is minimal.
What if an item fails? +
Failed items are tagged “Do Not Use” and removed from service immediately. You’ll receive the failure reason and recommended next steps. While we don’t repair equipment, we coordinate retesting once it’s fixed or replaced.
Do I receive a report? +
Yes—every visit includes a digital asset register, failure report, and schedule for the next due dates. Need a specific format? Let us know and we’ll export to match your systems.
How often do items need testing? +
Intervals follow AS/NZS 3760 guidelines and depend on usage and environment. Construction sites require three-month testing, hospitality and workshops are typically six months, and office environments can be every 12 months or longer. We’ll confirm the right cadence for your assets.
